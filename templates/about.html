Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Siva K, Triowise began as a major project during his BSc Computer Science at S.T. Hindu College, Nagercoil. What started as a college project has evolved into a fully functional e-commerce marketplace.

Leveraging skills from his Diploma in Full Stack Java and expertise in Zoho Creator, Siva built this platform to demonstrate how modern web technologies can create seamless shopping experiences. The name "Triowise" represents the three pillars of wisdom in e-commerce: Trust, Quality, and Innovation.
