FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I track my order?

You can track your order from the 'My Orders' section in your account. You'll also receive tracking updates via email and SMS with real-time delivery status.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy for most products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Simply initiate a return from your orders page.

How do I become a seller?

Click on 'Become a Seller' in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form. Our team will review your application and contact you within 48 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and Cash on Delivery for eligible orders.
