Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order from the 'My Orders' section in your account. You'll also receive tracking updates via email and SMS.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy for most products. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

How do I become a seller?

Click on 'Become a Seller' in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form. Our team will contact you within 48 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and Cash on Delivery.
